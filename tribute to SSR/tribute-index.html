Sushant Singh Rajput (21 January 1986 – 14 June 2020) was an Indian actor who is best known for his work in Hindi cinema. Rajput started his career with television serials. His debut show was Star Plus's romantic drama Kis Desh Mein Hai Meraa Dil (2008), followed by a starring role in Zee TV's soap opera Pavitra Rishta (2009–2011).

Achievments

In 2010,2011 Indian Television Academy Awards, BIG Star Entertainment Awards, Gold Awards, FICCI Frames Excellence Honours Awards for Best Television Actor (Male). for Pavitra Rishta 2014 Producers Guild Film Awards, Screen Awards for Best Male Debut for Kai Po Che! 2016,2017 Screen Awards, Indian Film Festival of Melbourne for Best Actor for M.S Dhoni: The Untold Story.

Personal Life

Rajput's personal relationships have been the subject of widespread media attention. In 2011 , Rajput had publicly proposed to his Pavitra Rishta co-star Ankita Lokhande during a national broadcast of the dance competition show Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa; both of them had participated in the show as competing contestants. The couple were dating after their first meeting on the sets of Pavitra Rishta, where they were incidentally cast in the role of husband and wife to each other. Since 2012 , they began living as cohabiting partners. In the following period, reports of an imminent marriage between the two occasionally kept cropping up in the media. In an interview, Rajput had stated that marriages and cohabitation were no different from each other and that the country was full of hypocrites who made it seem like the former was sacred and the latter was a crime.

After Rajput's death, Chakraborty confirmed that she was his girlfriend and that he had refused medication, relying instead on yoga and meditation for relief. According to Rajput's father, the actor planned to marry in 2021 but did not reveal his intended bride; Ankita Lokhande was the only girlfriend of Rajput that his father had known about.

Death

On 14 June 2020, Rajput, aged 34, was found dead, hanging from the ceiling fan in his home in Bandra, Mumbai. He had reportedly shown signs of clinical depression and was suffering from bipolar disorder. The Mumbai Police commenced an investigation, stating that the death was being treated as a suicide. The postmortem report stated that the cause of death was "asphyxia due to hanging," and called it a "clear case of suicide." The autopsy doctors placed time of death at 10 to 12 hours before postmortem examination on 14 June at 11:30 p.m.—meaning between 11:30 a.m. and 1:30 p.m. (Indian Standard Time). No foul play was found. The viscera report likewise ruled out foul play. On 25 July, Rajput's family lodged a first information report with police in Patna, where his father lives, accusing Rhea Chakraborty and five others of abetment of suicide.
